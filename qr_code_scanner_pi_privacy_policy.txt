Privacy Policy for QR Code Scanner PI  
Last updated: July 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how QR Code Scanner PI ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage
- QR Code Scanner PI does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
- The app scans QR codes and barcodes using the device’s camera and processes the data locally.
- Scanned results can be saved locally on the device for history purposes.
- No scanned data is transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

2. Permissions Required
QR Code Scanner PI requests only the permissions necessary for its functionality:

- Camera access to display the live preview and scan codes in real-time.
- No access is made to location, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive information.

3. Third-Party Services
- The app works fully offline and does not connect to any external services.
- It does not transmit or receive data from the internet.
- No data is shared with third parties.

4. Security & Compliance
- Since no user data is collected or transmitted, no additional security mechanisms are required beyond local device security.
- The app complies with Google Play’s Data Safety policies.
- QR Code Scanner PI does not target or knowingly interact with children under 13.

5. Updates & Contact
This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be reflected on the app’s Play Store page and/or website.
