Privacy Policy for Android Sensor Info
Last updated: July 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Android Sensor Info ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage
- Android Sensor Info does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
- The app displays real-time sensor information available on the device (e.g., accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, light, pressure, orientation).
- No sensor data is transmitted off-device or stored beyond the current session.

2. Permissions Required
- The app uses the Android system sensor framework and does not request special permissions like location, storage, or internet.
- All sensor readings are handled locally through Android's built-in APIs.
- No personally identifiable information is accessed.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising
- Android Sensor Info does not use third-party SDKs, analytics, or external APIs.
- The app does not serve ads or include external content providers.
- No data is shared with advertisers or third parties.

4. Security & Compliance
- As no user data is collected or stored, no special security handling is required.
- The app complies with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
- Android Sensor Info is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from children under 13.
