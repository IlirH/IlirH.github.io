Privacy Policy for QR Code Generator PI  
Last updated: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how QR Code Generator PI ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage
- QR Code Generator PI does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
- The app allows users to generate and optionally save QR codes locally on their device.
- No information is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
- Saved QR codes remain on the user’s device and are not accessed or processed by us.

2. Permissions Required
QR Code Generator PI requests only minimal permissions necessary for functionality:

- Storage access (MediaStore) to save QR code images to the user’s gallery.
- No access is made to contacts, location, microphone, camera, or personal files.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising
- QR Code Generator PI does not include ads, trackers, or third-party SDKs.
- It does not use analytics or connect to any online services or APIs.
- No data is shared with advertisers or any third parties.

4. Security & Compliance
- Since no user data is collected, stored, or transmitted, no special security mechanisms are required.
- The app complies with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
- QR Code Generator PI does not target or knowingly interact with children under 13.

5. Updates & Contact
This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be posted on the app’s store page and/or website.
