Privacy Policy for Dead Pixel Fixer PI
Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dead Pixel Fixer PI ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. 
By using the app, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Data Collection & Usage
Dead Pixel Fixer PI is a local utility tool designed to help identify and attempt to repair display anomalies like dead pixels and screen burn-in.
- We do not collect, transmit, intercept, or store personal data, usage logs, or analytics on external servers.
- The app operates entirely offline on your device.
- Any app preferences (such as Dark Mode settings) and your specific user status (such as "Early User" flags) are stored securely and locally on your device using an encrypted database.

2. Hardware & Display Control
- To provide its core functionality, the app temporarily adjusts your device's screen brightness to its maximum level and prevents the device from sleeping during an active repair cycle. 
- These actions are performed entirely locally using standard Android system tools. 
- We do not track, record, or transmit when, how long, or how often you use the repair or diagnostic cycles.

3. Permissions
The app is built to prioritize your privacy and requests absolutely zero dangerous permissions:
- It does not require or request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or personal files.
- It does not require an active internet connection to perform its display diagnostic and repair functions.

4. Third-Party Services
- This version of the app does not use third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling services.
- No personal data is shared with or sold to third parties.

5. Security
- We employ local, industry-standard database encryption (SQLCipher) to protect the user flags and settings stored locally on your device, preventing unauthorized access to your app data.

6. Children's Privacy
- Dead Pixel Fixer PI does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
