You are in control of your data when using Beam AI Companion. This page explains how you can delete your account, chat history, and companion profiles.

Delete Your Account

To request full account deletion (including your email login, saved chats, and custom companions), please email us at: support@infernusapps.com.

Once processed, all of your account data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Delete Chat History

You can delete individual chats directly in the app. Deleted chats are permanently removed from both your device and our servers.

Delete Companion Profiles

Custom AI companions you create can be deleted from within the app at any time. Once deleted, they are permanently removed.

Need Help?

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@infernusapps.com
