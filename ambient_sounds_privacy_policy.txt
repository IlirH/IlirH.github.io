Privacy Policy for Ambient Sounds
Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ambient Sounds ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage
- Ambient Sounds does not collect or store personal data directly for its own purposes.
- However, the app uses third-party services (such as Google AdMob) that may automatically collect certain data, as described in the section below.
- The app plays locally packaged relaxing and ambient sounds for user wellbeing, focus, and sleep.

2. Local Data & Storage
- The app stores simple preference data (such as your "Dark Mode" setting) locally on your device.
- This data is stored in a secure, encrypted database to ensure your preferences remain private.
- This data never leaves your device and cannot be accessed by other apps.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising
- Ambient Sounds uses third-party SDKs specifically for advertising (Google AdMob).
- Google AdMob is provided by Google LLC and may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.
- The app displays ads to support development. Our advertising partner automatically collects certain data to serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.
- This data includes device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), approximate location, app interactions, and diagnostic data (like crash logs and performance metrics).
- Depending on your region, you will be presented with a consent form to manage your personalized advertising preferences.

4. Security & Compliance
- While your app preferences are processed safely and locally on your device, we ensure any data processed by our advertising partners is handled securely.
- The app complies with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
- Ambient Sounds does not target or collect data from children under 13.

5. Updates & Contact
This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be posted on the app’s store page and website.
