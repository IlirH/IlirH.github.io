Privacy Policy for Touch Tester PI
Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Touch Tester PI ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Data Collection & Usage
Touch Tester PI is a diagnostic utility built to map and test your device's touch screen. We do not directly collect, harvest, transmit, or store personal data for our own use. Your app preferences, including theme settings and early-user status, are securely saved locally on your device within an encrypted local database.

2. Diagnostic Data & Touch Input
To deliver its main features, the app tracks your touch inputs (such as taps, swipes, and multi-touch gestures) strictly in real-time on your screen to identify dead zones. We do not record, log, intercept, or transmit your touch coordinates or gesture data outside of the app's active session. The data is only used visually to map your screen's hardware health in real-time.

3. Permissions
Touch Tester PI is designed to be non-intrusive. The core diagnostic engine requires no special device permissions to successfully map your display dead zones.

4. Security & Compliance
- We utilize local database encryption to keep your internal app configurations and preferences secure on your physical device. 
- Touch Tester PI fully complies with Google Play's Data Safety policies.

5. Children's Privacy
- Touch Tester PI does not knowingly target, solicit, or collect personal data from anyone under the age of 13.
