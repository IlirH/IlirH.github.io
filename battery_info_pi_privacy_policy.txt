Privacy Policy for Battery Info PI
Last updated: March 5, 2026 

This Privacy Policy explains how Battery Info PI ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. 
By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage
- Battery Info PI does not collect, store, or share any personal data for external purposes. 
- The app reads battery-related information from the Android system API and app usage logs via the Usage Access permission, displaying them to the user in real-time. 
- Information accessed includes battery percentage, charging status, voltage, current, temperature, battery health, estimated battery life, and app-specific power usage. 
- Your battery diagnostics and app-specific power usage logs are not sent to our external servers, third parties, or stored externally. 

The app does not track, store, or transmit your battery diagnostics or app-specific power usage outside your device. 

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising 
- Battery Info PI uses third-party SDKs specifically for advertising (Google AdMob). 
- The app displays ads to support development. Our advertising partner automatically collects certain data to serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. This data includes device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), approximate location, app interactions, and diagnostic data (like crash logs and performance metrics). 
- Depending on your region, you will be presented with a consent form to manage your personalized advertising preferences. 

3. Security & Compliance
- While your battery and power usage data are processed safely and locally on your device, we ensure any data processed by our advertising partners is handled securely. 
- The app complies with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
- Battery Info PI does not target or knowingly collect data from children under 13. 

4. Updates & Contact
This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be posted on the app's store page.
