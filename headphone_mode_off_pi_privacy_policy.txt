Headphone Mode Off PI is a utility designed to fix audio routing issues on your device.

2. Audio Permissions & Functionality
To function correctly, the app interacts with your device's audio hardware in the following ways:
- Audio Settings: We use the "Modify Audio Settings" permission to force audio output to your built-in speaker or headphones.

This process happens entirely on your device. We do not record audio, listen to calls, or analyze sound.
