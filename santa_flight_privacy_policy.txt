Privacy Policy for Santa Flight
Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Santa Flight ("Santa Flight", "we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Santa Flight is designed to be a simple, lightweight, and privacy-friendly casual game.

- Santa Flight is a single-player arcade game where users control a character by tapping to fly and avoid obstacles.
- All gameplay logic, scoring, and interactions occur locally on the device.
- The game does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.
- No accounts, sign-ins, or user profiles are required.

2. Offline Usage

- Santa Flight works fully offline.
- No internet connection is required to play the game.
- No data is transmitted over the network during gameplay.

3. Permissions

Santa Flight only uses system access that is necessary for the core functionality of the game.
No access to personal information is required to play.

4. Data Storage

- Game-related data (such as scores or basic settings) is stored locally on the device only.
- Local data is used solely to support gameplay functionality.
- This data is not shared with any external systems.
- Users may clear all locally stored data at any time via the device’s system settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Santa Flight does not share user data with third parties.

6. Data Security

- All data handled by Santa Flight remains on the user’s device.
- Standard Android platform security mechanisms are used to protect locally stored data.
- Santa Flight does not transmit user data externally.

7. Children’s Privacy

- Santa Flight does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
- As no personal data is collected or transmitted, the game is suitable for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the game’s functionality changes or to comply with legal requirements.
Any updates will be reflected in the app’s store listing or accompanying documentation.
