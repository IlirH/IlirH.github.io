Privacy Policy for Headphone Mode Off PI
Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Headphone Mode Off PI ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage
- Headphone Mode Off PI does not collect or store personal data directly for its own purposes. However, the app uses third-party services (such as Google AdMob) that may automatically collect certain data, as described in the section below.
- The app operates primarily offline to fix audio routing issues on your device. We do not access your location, contacts, or files. 

2. Audio Permissions & Local Data
To function correctly, the app interacts with your device's audio hardware locally:
- Audio Settings: We use the "Modify Audio Settings" permission to force audio output to your built-in speaker or headphones.
- Hardware Detection: The app detects whether a headphone jack or USB audio device is plugged in to display the correct status. 
- Audio Testing: The "Test Audio" feature allows you to play a system notification sound to verify that the speaker fix is working. This happens locally and does not access your personal music library.
- Local Storage: The app stores simple preference data (such as your "Dark Mode" setting) locally on your device. This data is stored in a secure, encrypted database to ensure your preferences remain private. This data never leaves your device and cannot be accessed by other apps.
- We do not record audio, listen to calls, or analyze sound.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising
- Headphone Mode Off PI uses third-party SDKs specifically for advertising (Google AdMob).
- Google AdMob is provided by Google LLC and may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.
- The app displays ads to support development. Our advertising partner automatically collects certain data to serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. This data includes device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), approximate location, app interactions, and diagnostic data (like crash logs and performance metrics).
- Depending on your region, you will be presented with a consent form to manage your personalized advertising preferences.

4. Security & Compliance
- While your audio routing preferences are processed safely and locally on your device, we ensure any data processed by our advertising partners is handled securely.
- The app complies with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. 

5. Updates & Contact
This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be posted on the app's store page.
